Admission Process

Admission Steps

The admission process at our school is simple and transparent. Admissions for the new session begin in [Month Name].

  1. Obtain the admission form and prospectus from the school office.
  2. Submit the duly filled form with required documents to the office.
  3. Participate in a general entrance test and/or interview.
  4. Upon selection, confirm your admission by paying the fees.

Required Documents

Submission of copies of the following documents is mandatory at the time of admission:

  • Student's Birth Certificate
  • Marksheet / Report Card of the previous class
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school
  • 2 passport-sized photos of the student
  • Citizenship certificate of the parent/guardian
